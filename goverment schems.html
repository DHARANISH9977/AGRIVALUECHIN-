Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
Objective: Provides income support to all landholding farmer families. Benefit: Rs. 6,000 per year in three equal installments directly transferred to the bank accounts of beneficiaries.



Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
Objective: Provides insurance coverage and financial support to farmers in the event of crop failure. Benefit: Insurance coverage for both Rabi and Kharif crops at a very low premium.



Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
Objective: Provides farmers with timely access to credit for their agricultural needs. Benefit: Short-term credit at lower interest rates for crop production and other farming expenses.



Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)
Objective: To enhance irrigation coverage and improve water use efficiency. Components: Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Programme (AIBP): Focuses on the completion of ongoing irrigation projects. Har Khet Ko Pani: Ensures water to every farm. Per Drop More Crop: Promotes precision irrigation
