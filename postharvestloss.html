Pick at the right time: Harvest crops when they are ready, not too early or late.



Handle carefully: Be gentle with fruits and vegetables to avoid damage.



Store in a cool, dry place: Keep crops in a cool, dry spot to stop them from spoiling.



Dry before storing: Make sure grains and seeds are fully dry before storing.



Protect from pests: Keep insects and animals away from stored crops.



Sell or use quickly: Get crops to market or use them soon after harvest.
